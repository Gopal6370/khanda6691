# E-Commerce Sales Performance Analysis & Statistical Validation

## Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing E-Commerce customer purchase data to uncover business insights and validate findings using statistical methods. The objective is to transform raw transaction data into actionable recommendations that support business growth and decision-making.

---

## Objectives

* Analyze customer purchasing behavior.
* Identify high-performing product categories.
* Evaluate sales performance across cities.
* Understand customer demographics.
* Validate business insights using hypothesis testing.

## Key Insights

* Electronics category generated the highest sales.
* Major cities contributed significantly to overall revenue.
* Customers aged 25–35 formed the largest customer segment.
* Statistical testing confirmed significant differences in spending behavior.

---

## Statistical Validation

### Hypothesis Test

H0: There is no significant difference in purchase amounts between male and female customers.

H1: There is a significant difference in purchase amounts between male and female customers.

Result:

* T-Statistic = 2.9552
* P-Value = 0.00847

Conclusion:
The p-value is less than 0.05, indicating a statistically significant difference in spending behavior.
